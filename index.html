Fine Art Photography

One of the main aspects of my work over the last few years has been an attempt to debate and rethink the means in which photographic works are presented and viewed. This debate tries to create experiences that confront the viewer with the vulnerable and unstable state of contemporary images and with their complex ontological status, while paralleling these images with subversion and reflection on artistic photography as a commodity.
